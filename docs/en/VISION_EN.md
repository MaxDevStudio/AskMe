# Vision — AskMe & MaxDevStudio

**One-sentence mission**  
AskMe — це диригент, який перетворює хаос AI-агентів на організовану студію, де одна людина може керувати десятками розумних помічників без страху втратити контроль.

We are not building another chatbot.  
We are building an **AI-native operating layer** for creators, developers, and small teams who want to work faster, safer, and smarter.

### Core Belief
The future belongs to those who can orchestrate intelligence — not just use it.  
One human + many specialized AI agents + one intelligent conductor = a studio that never sleeps.

### Our Values
- Control always stays with the human, not the model  
- Security & isolation above everything (no agent gets more rights than needed)  
- Locality by default (maximum capabilities without cloud dependency)  
- Open core, closed critical security parts  
- Simplicity for the user, complexity under the hood

### Who We Build For
- Solo developers & freelancers (UpWork, indie game dev, 3D makers)  
- Small studios (2–10 people) who want to scale without hiring an army  
- Sysadmins & DevOps tired of routine  
- Creators who want to automate boring parts (render, editing, backups)

### What We Are Creating
- AskMe Free — open-source CLI brain for every Linux user  
- AskMe Pro (AIO) — closed-source Rust powerhouse (AI-Native AD)  
- PowerDev — beautiful AI-first desktop distro  
- LiteDev — rock-solid server/VPS edition

### Long-Term Dream (2027–2030)
A global network of independent creators running their own AI studios.  
Each studio has:
- One human captain
- One AskMe Pro conductor
- Dozens of specialized agents
- Full control over local hardware, VMs, cloud, remote machines

No more "AI gone rogue".  
No more manual config hell.  
No more "I wish I had a team".

Just one command, one voice, one brain — and the work gets done.

### Why Now?
Agents are already here.  
But no one knows how to safely manage hundreds of them across machines and clouds.  
We will be the first to give people real control over their AI workforce.

MaxDevStudio — where solo creators become unstoppable studios.
